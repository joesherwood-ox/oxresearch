Services

Each engagement is tailored to your context and constraints, with an emphasis on clarity, practicality, and measurable outcomes.

1) Research & Program Evaluation

What we deliver

  • Mixed-methods evaluations (surveys, interviews, focus groups, administrative data)
  • Outcomes and process assessments
  • Benchmarking and environmental scans
  • Dashboards and stakeholder-ready reporting

Typical engagements

  • Grant-funded program evaluations
  • Workforce and training impact studies
  • Policy or regulatory process reviews
  • Community and stakeholder engagement assessments

Outcomes

  • What’s working, what needs adjustment, and where to invest next
  • Credible evidence for boards, funders, and leadership
  • Clear recommendations with phased action plans

Example deliverables

  • Evaluation plan and logic model
  • Instrument design and translation (EN/ES)
  • Technical report + executive summary
  • Slide deck for leadership and funders
