About

Who we serve

Mission-driven organizations across federal, nonprofit, higher education, and private sectors.

Experience

Cross-sector consulting in evaluation, workforce strategy, organizational performance, and change management; bilingual research delivery (English/Spanish).

Our work is guided by

  • Fairness & Dignity – People deserve respectful systems and clear expectations. We operate under the assumption that organizations hire good people and that our work should empower the whole workforce.
  • Trust & Confidentiality – Confidential interviews and focus groups require psychological safety. We will never provide information intended to justify termination or severance.
  • Sustainability & Stewardship – We prefer work aligned with long-term planetary well-being.
  • Empowerment & Community – Solutions should strengthen local communities and elevate every member of the organization.
  • Scientific Rigor & Clarity – Methods matter; so does transparency and actionable reporting.

Mission

Align evidence, strategy, and values to improve outcomes for people and communities.

How we work

Start simple, learn quickly, scale what works. Practical deliverables, minimal jargon, measurable impact.
